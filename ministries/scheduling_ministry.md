# Scheduling Ministry  
## Runtime / Ministries

---

## 1. Purpose

The Scheduling Ministry manages time‑based actions, reminders, and routines.  
It ensures Edencore supports the user’s daily flow.

---

## 2. Responsibilities

- reminders  
- routines  
- timed actions  
- calendar coordination  
- habit support  

---

## 3. Boundaries

The Scheduling Ministry does not:

- interpret meaning  
- manage devices  
- override safety  

---

## 4. Constitutional Statement

**Scheduling must be reliable, predictable, and user‑centred.**
