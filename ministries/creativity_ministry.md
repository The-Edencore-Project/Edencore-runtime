# Creativity Ministry  
## Runtime / Ministries

---

## 1. Purpose

The Creativity Ministry generates ideas, solutions, and imaginative outputs.  
It supports improvisation, design, and lateral thinking.

---

## 2. Responsibilities

- creative problem‑solving  
- generating ideas  
- producing content  
- supporting improvisation  
- enhancing user creativity  

---

## 3. Boundaries

The Creativity Ministry does not:

- override safety  
- interpret meaning  
- manage devices  

---

## 4. Constitutional Statement

**Creativity must serve clarity, purpose, and user empowerment.**
