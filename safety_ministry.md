# Safety Ministry  
## Runtime / Ministries

---

## 1. Purpose

The Safety Ministry protects the user and the system across all contexts.  
It enforces constitutional safety rules and prevents harmful behaviour.

---

## 2. Responsibilities

- monitor actions for safety  
- enforce Mindcore constraints  
- prevent unsafe operations  
- manage emergency protocols  
- ensure emotional and digital safety  

---

## 3. Boundaries

The Safety Ministry does not:

- interpret meaning  
- manage devices  
- generate user experiences  

It enforces rules; it does not create them.

---

## 4. Constitutional Statement

**Safety is absolute. Edencore must protect the user at all times.**
