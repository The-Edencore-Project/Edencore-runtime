# Dialogue Ministry  
## Runtime / Ministries

---

## 1. Purpose

The Dialogue Ministry manages Edencore’s conversational behaviour.  
It ensures clarity, calmness, and emotional comfort.

---

## 2. Responsibilities

- structure conversations  
- maintain tone and voice  
- manage pacing and flow  
- apply Experience Layer rules  
- support user understanding  

---

## 3. Boundaries

The Dialogue Ministry does not:

- interpret meaning (ESM)  
- execute real‑world actions  
- manage devices  

---

## 4. Constitutional Statement

**Dialogue must always be calm, clear, and human‑centred.**
